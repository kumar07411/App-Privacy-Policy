Privacy Policy

Sherpa TTS (package: com.sherpa.tts)
Effective date: 14 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sherpa TTS Android application ("the App", "we") handles your information. Sherpa TTS is an offline text-to-speech app: all speech generation happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect Directly

None. The App itself does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, phone number or location, and does not include any analytics or tracking code of its own. The text you type and the audio you generate are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to us or anyone else.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App shows advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. To serve and measure ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • IP address (which can imply approximate location)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size, language)
  • Ad interaction data (which ads were viewed or clicked)

This data is collected and processed by Google and its ad partners, not by us. For details, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in AdMob
  • How Google uses information from apps that use its services

3. Your Consent Choices (EEA, UK, Switzerland & US States)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland, the App shows a consent form (Google's User Messaging Platform) on first launch, before any ads are requested. You may consent to or decline personalized advertising; if consent is declined where it is required, ads are not requested.

If you are located in a US state with an applicable privacy law (such as California), you may be shown options to limit the processing of your information for advertising.

You can also reset or delete your Advertising ID at any time in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads (or Settings → Privacy → Ads).

4. Files Saved by the App

When you save generated audio (WAV/MP3), the file is written to your device's shared storage in the Download/Sherpa TTS folder using Android's MediaStore. These files stay on your device only and are fully under your control — the App never uploads them. Sharing a file with another app happens only when you explicitly use the share function.

5. Model Download

On first launch, the App downloads the Kokoro text-to-speech model files (approximately 310 MB) from Hugging Face (huggingface.co), a third-party file hosting service. As with any internet download, Hugging Face's servers can see your IP address during the download. No other information is sent. See the Hugging Face Privacy Policy.

The App does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, or files created by other apps.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade or share any personal information, because we do not collect any. The only third parties that receive data are Google (for advertising, as described in Section 2) and Hugging Face (your IP address during the model download, as described in Section 5).

8. Data Security & Retention

Since all speech processing is local and we operate no servers and no user database, there is no personal data for us to store, lose or breach. Data handled by Google is retained according to Google's retention policies.

9. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising is served through Google AdMob in compliance with its policies.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your region (GDPR in the EEA/UK, CCPA/CPRA in California, and similar laws elsewhere), you may have the right to access, correct or delete personal data, to withdraw consent, and to object to processing. Because the App itself does not collect personal data, such requests generally concern data held by Google — you can exercise those rights through Google My Ad Center and your device's Advertising ID settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this same URL with an updated effective date. Significant changes will be reflected in the App's store listing.
