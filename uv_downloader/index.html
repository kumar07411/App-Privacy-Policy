Privacy Policy

UV Downloader  •  Last updated: July 19, 2026

Thank you for using UV Downloader ("the App"). Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App does and does not collect, and how your data is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not track your browsing or download history.
  • We do not see, store or transmit your passwords.
  • We do not upload your downloaded files anywhere.
  • We do not use our own analytics or tracking SDKs.

2. Data Stored on Your Device Only

All data created by the App stays 100% on your device:

  • Downloaded files — saved to your device's Downloads folder, fully under your control.
  • App settings — such as download quality and folder preferences, stored locally.
  • Login sessions (optional) — if you choose to log in to X or Instagram, the login happens directly on the official website inside a secure in-app browser. Only the session cookies are saved, locally and privately, on your device. They are excluded from device backups and are never uploaded anywhere. We never see or store your username or password. You can log out anytime, which permanently deletes these cookies.

3. Advertising

The App shows advertisements to keep it free. Ads are provided by Start.io (formerly StartApp), a third-party advertising network. When an ad is loaded or shown, Start.io may automatically collect certain device information, such as:

  • Device type, model and operating system version
  • IP address and approximate (coarse) location derived from it
  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID)
  • Network information and ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • This data is collected and processed by Start.io, not by us — we never see or store it.
  • It is used to serve, personalize and measure ads.
  • You can reset or delete your Advertising ID anytime in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads.
  • For details, please read the Start.io Privacy Policy.

4. Internet Access

The App connects to the internet only to:

  • Fetch and download the media content you request from the links you provide.
  • Load advertisements from the Start.io ad network (see Section 3).
  • Check for App updates (see Section 5).

No other data leaves your device.

5. In-App Updates

The App periodically checks GitHub (github.com) to see whether a newer version of the App is available. This is a simple version check — no personal data is sent. If you choose to update, the new version is downloaded directly from GitHub over a secure connection and installed with your confirmation.

6. Permissions

  • Storage / Media — required to save downloaded files to your device.
  • Notifications — used to show download progress and completion status.
  • Install unknown apps (optional) — asked only when you install an in-app update; used solely to install the new version of this App.
  • Network state — used to retry downloads and ads when your internet connection returns.

7. Third-Party Content

The App lets you download content from third-party platforms (such as YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and X). These platforms have their own terms of service and privacy policies. You are responsible for ensuring your use of downloaded content complies with the applicable platform's terms and your local copyright laws. Downloaded content is intended for personal, offline use only.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from them. The third-party ad network (Start.io) is instructed to serve ads in accordance with applicable children's privacy laws (such as COPPA).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
